About This Site

To showcase my knowledge of HTML this practice website will be used as a lore bible for characters of my webnovel concept called Luxuria which is a dark romance story between Sienna Clarke and Kieran Moretti. This website will include character profiles, world-building, and other relevant plot and story information.

Character profiles:

  1. Sienna Clarke - The FMC. Was majoring in investigative journalism until taken by Kieran.
  2. Kieran Moretti - The MMC. A man of a wealthy family, who are rivals to the Castellano family. Has been "madly in love" With Sienna 5 years before she even knew him.
  3. Isla Beaumont - Was also taken in by Kieran but for a different reason. She serves as the maid in Kieran's mansion. she's more like a sibling to him though.
  4. Felix - Kieran's right hand. His factotum. No task is beneath him. He's even played as his butler. Kieran sees Felix as a Nephew.
